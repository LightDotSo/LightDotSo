## Flow

1. **Creation of Light Smart Wallet** - The user creates a Light Smart Wallet by deploying a smart contract on the Light Protocol.
2. **Deposit of Assets** - The user deposits assets into the Light Smart Wallet.
3. **Execution of Transactions** - The user executes transactions on the Light Protocol, experiencing what chain abstraction should feel like.

## Deposit & Escrow & Withdraw

1. **Deposit** - The user deposits assets into the Light Smart Wallet.
2. **Escrow** - The user can further escrow the assets in the Light Protocol for fronted services.
3. **Withdraw** - The user can withdraw the assets from the Light Smart Wallet or Light Protocol.
