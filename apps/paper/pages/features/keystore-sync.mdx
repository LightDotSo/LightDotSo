# Keystore Sync in Light

![keystore-sync](https://assets.light.so/paper/keystore-sync.png)

:::tip[Keystore Sync]
Keystore Sync streamlines the synchronization of keystore wallets across multiple blockchain networks.
It grants users the convenience of switching between chains without the burden of individual wallet updates.
With Keystore Sync, users can securely manage their wallets on different chains using a single address, reducing complexity and enhancing security.
:::
