# Light Layer in Light

![light-layer](https://assets.light.so/paper/light-layer.png)

:::tip[Enabling Chain Abstraction and Programmability]
The Light Layer offers an EVM-compatible Layer 2 solution that supports the execution of programmatic intents.
It enables users to initiate cross-chain transactions that interact seamlessly across a multitude of chains.
With a focus on programmability, the Light Layer equips users to actualize their desired outcomes by abstracting the complexities of cross-chain dynamics.
:::
