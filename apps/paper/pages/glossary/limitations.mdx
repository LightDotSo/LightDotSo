# Known Limitations

These are some known limitations of underlying protocols that have affected the design of the system:

## EVM Related

- Only EOAs have the ability to initiate transactions
- Account abstraction is not baked into the EVM protocol

## 4337 Related

- Smart wallets not being able to act as signers for other smart wallets
- Gas is assumed to be priced real-time, bundlers will not accept lower prices in the mempool
- Hard to tell the contents of the transaction in `eth_sign` if the digest is a user operation hash
