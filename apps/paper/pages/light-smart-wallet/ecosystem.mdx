# Ecosystem Support

## Overview

Lightactively supports a range of ecosystem initiatives, fostering compatibility and ensuring alignment with the broader EVM landscape.

## ERC4337 - Account Abstraction

Lightsupports the ERC-4337 standard, enabling a unified approach to account abstraction.
This allows users to easily switch between infrastructure providers without facing vendor lock-in.

## ERC7579 - Minimal Modular Smart Accounts

Lightsupports the ERC-7579 standard for smart accounts, allowing the integration and management of modular plugins.
LightSmart Accounts can utilize any module compatible with ERC-7579, promoting versatility and broad module support.
